    About Me

    My name is Miki Aso.

    I am working at OYO RMS Corporation as an earthquake analyst. Now I am concerned with analyzing earthquakes hazard and their risk in Japan.

    Also, my scientific interest is about the source of earthquake, especially the difference or the similarity between slow and fast earthquakes.
